# Dragon Jet
The Dragon Jet is the most powerful thruster in KartMakers, but at a cost. It is by far the heaviest and bulkiest thruster. Combine this with a long spool time, its use must be well thought out.

## Stats
Weight: 22.5kg

Power: 300
